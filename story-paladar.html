A Real Winter, 2012

Paladar Fumior Salon is South Brisbane's tiniest little cult cafe. Purveyors of quality plantation coffee and merchants of the finest Cuban cigars, Paladar lends an air of curiosity and romance to its concrete surrounds. A little red bunker in a grey part of town. Filip, the owner of Paladar, asked me to take some photographs of the shop for their upcoming website overhaul. Australia's new tobacco legislation requires the establishment and its website to be free of any imagery depicting cigars or related products, so moody coffee and cozy interior pictures it is.
